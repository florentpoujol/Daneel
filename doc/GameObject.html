GameObject.BroadcastMessage (gameObject, functionName, data)
Tries to call a method with the provided name on all the scriptedBehaviors attached to the gameObject or any of its descendants. The data argument can be nil or a table you want the method to receive as its first (and only) argument. If none of the scripteBehaviors attached to the gameObject or its children have a method matching the provided name, nothing happens.

Parameters:

  • gameObject: (GameObject) The gameObject.
  • functionName: (string) The method name.
  • data: [optional] (table) The data to pass along the method call.

GameObject.GetChild (gameObject, name, recursive)
Alias of GameObject:FindChild(). Find the first gameObject's child with the provided name.

Parameters:

  • gameObject: (GameObject) The gameObject.

  • recursive: [optional default=false] (boolean) Search for the child in all descendants instead of just the first generation.

    (GameObject) The child or nil if none is found.
GameObject.GetChildren (gameObject, recursive, includeSelf)

    (table) The children.
